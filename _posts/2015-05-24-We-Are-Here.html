--- layout: post title: "7,000 Times a Minute" date: 2015-05-17 15:49:48 categories: Visual-Communication --- 7,000 Times a Minute* is a book on tattooing in Dublin. The book includes information on how tattooing works, interviews with people with tattoos and a photographic documentation of tattooing from start to finish.

* A tattoo needle enters approximately 7,000 times a minute.
