Living in Dublin, Ireland, I have recently graduated from the Textile and Surface Design degree course in NCAD. Previously graduating from Visual Communications at IADT, Dun Laoghaire, in 2012. I am very interested in craft based design and work with screen print, letterpress printing and weave. Please feel free to contact me about my work or any potential projects you may have.
