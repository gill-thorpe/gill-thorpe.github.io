Hello! My name is Gill Thorpe and I am an Irish designer currently living and interning in London, United Kingdom. I graduated from the National College of Art and Design, Dublin, Ireland in June 2015 with a BA Hons Degree in Textile and Surface Design. I also have a BA Hons Degree in Visual Communication from the Institute of Art, Design and Technology, Dun Laoghaire, Ireland graduating in 2012. I love to work in different forms of print and craft based design. Please feel free to contact me for more information about my work, collaborations, or just to say hello!
